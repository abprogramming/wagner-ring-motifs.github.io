Welcome to my personal website!

I am a hungarian musician worked in various dark and experimental genres since 2006,
most prominently martial industrial, black metal, dark ambient, neofolk, neoclassical, EBM and other underground and electronic genres.
Please use the image links to access my releases from Bandcamp!

I am also a devout collector and maniac of underground and classical music, which are the main inspiration for my works:
operas from Richard Wagner and other classical music, mostly from the romantic and 20th century era; 90's black and heavy metal;
anything from the so-called "dark" scene, from 80's goth rock and EBM to post-industrial and neofolk;
70's progressive rock, Kraftwerk and Tangerine Dream; and sometimes even lighter electronic genres like acid and IDM.
