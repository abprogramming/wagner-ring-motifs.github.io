Welcome to my personal website!

I am a hungarian musician worked in various dark and experimental genres since 2006,
including dark ambient, black metal, martial industrial, neofolk, neoclassical, EBM and other underground and electronic genres.

I am also a devout collector and maniac of underground and classical music, I listen mostly to
Richard Wagner and classical music from the romantic and 20th century era; 90's black and heavy metal, Bal-Sagoth and Abigor in the first place;
anything from the so called "dark" scene, like 80's goth rock, The Mission, EBM, new wave, Dead Can Dance, Depeche Mode;
industrial pioneers like Laibach and Autopsia; 70's progressive rock, Kraftwerk and Tangerine Dream; and sometimes even lighter electronic genres like acid and IDM.
